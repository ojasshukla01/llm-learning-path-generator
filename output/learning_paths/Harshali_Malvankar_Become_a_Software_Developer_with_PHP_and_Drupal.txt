Week 1:
- Focus: PHP Fundamentals and Drupal Basics
- Resources:
  - PHP for Beginners: https://www.udemy.com/course/php-for-beginners/
  - Drupal 9 Fundamentals: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/getting-started/drupal-9-fundamentals
- Task: Complete the PHP for Beginners course and set up a local Drupal 9 development environment.

Week 2:
- Focus: PHP Intermediate Concepts and Drupal Theming
- Resources:
  - PHP Intermediate: https://www.w3schools.com/php/
  - Drupal Theming: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/theming/drupal-theming
- Task: Complete the PHP Intermediate course and create a simple Drupal 9 theme.

Week 3:
- Focus: Advanced PHP and Custom Drupal Modules
- Resources:
  - Advanced PHP: https://www.codecademy.com/learn/learn-php
  - Creating Custom Modules in Drupal 9: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/extending-drupal-9/creating-custom-modules
- Task: Implement a custom module in Drupal 9 based on the PHP concepts learned.

Week 4:
- Focus: Drupal 9 Module Development and Database Integration
- Resources:
  - Drupal 9 Module Development: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/extending-drupal-9/creating-custom-modules
  - Drupal 9 Database APIs: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/api/database-api/database-api
- Task: Create a Drupal 9 module that interacts with a local MySQL database.

Week 5:
- Focus: Drupal 9 Views and Entity API
- Resources:
  - Drupal 9 Views: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/creating-custom-content/creating-views
  - Drupal 9 Entity API: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/api/entity-api/entity-api
- Task: Develop a custom view and integrate it with an entity type of your choice.

Week 6:
- Focus: Drupal 9 Configuration Management and Drush
- Resources:
  - Drupal 9 Configuration Management: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/extending-drupal-8/developing-configurable-features
  - Drush Command Quick Reference: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/using-drush/quick-reference
- Task: Learn to use Drush for managing Drupal 9 configuration and deployments.

Week 7:
- Focus: Drupal 9 Performance Optimization and Security
- Resources:
  - Drupal 9 Performance Optimization: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/performance/performance-overview
  - Drupal 9 Security best practices: https://www.drupal.org/docs/8/security/best-practices
- Task: Improve the performance and security of your Drupal 9 site by implementing some best practices.

Week 8:
- Focus: Drupal 9 Testing and Contrib Modules
- Resources:
  - Drupal 9 Testing: https://www.drupal.org/docs/develop/testing-drupal/testing-drupal
  - Popular Drupal 9 Contrib Modules: https://www.drupal.org/project/modules
- Task: Implement automated testing for your Drupal 9 site and add some popular contrib modules to enhance its functionality.
