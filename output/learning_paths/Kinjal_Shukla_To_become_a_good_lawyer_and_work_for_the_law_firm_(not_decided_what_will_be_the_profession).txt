Week 1:
- Focus: Introduction to Law and Legal Studies
- Resources:
  - [Crash Course: U.S. Government & Civics: Introduction to Law](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1XvXGzXIYfs)
  - [Khan Academy: Introduction to Law](https://www.khanacademy.org/humanities/law-and-politics/introduction-to-law/a/introduction-to-law)
- Task: Watch the Crash Course video and complete the Khan Academy's Introduction to Law module. Write a one-page summary of the key principles and concepts you've learned, and discuss how they might apply to a legal career.

Week 2:
- Focus: Understanding the Legal System and Legal Research
- Resources:
  - [Legal Eagle: Understanding the Legal System](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0MhZMVN7L0o)
  - [Legal Research: Basic Research Strategies](https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/legal_research)
- Task: Watch the Legal Eagle video and familiarize yourself with the basic strategies for legal research. Conduct a legal research project using the Cornell Legal Information Institute's guide and find a case related to a current event or a topic of interest. Summarize the case in a one-page report, including the facts, the legal question, the decision, and the reasoning.

Week 3:
- Focus: Ethics and Professional Responsibility
- Resources:
  - [Crash Course: Ethics in Society: Ethics and Law](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JAqyO9cZBbg)
  - [American Bar Association: Model Rules of Professional Conduct](https://www.americanbar.org/groups/professional_responsibility/resources/model_rules_of_professional_conduct/)
- Task: Watch the Crash Course video on Ethics in Society and review the American Bar Association's Model Rules of Professional Conduct. Write a reflection on the importance of ethics in the legal profession and provide a real-life example of a legal ethical dilemma and how you would approach it.

Week 4:
- Focus: Legal Writing and Communication Skills
- Resources:
  - [Legal Writing Pro: Basic Legal Writing Skills](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nTUZK3JE1Ic)
  - [The Legal Writing Institute: Legal Writing Resources](https://www.legalwriting.org/resources/)
- Task: Watch the Legal Writing Pro video and explore the resources available on the Legal Writing Institute's website. Write a 500-word memorandum explaining a hypothetical legal issue, including a statement of facts, a legal argument, and a conclusion.

Week 5:
- Focus: Introduction to Constitutional Law
- Resources:
  - [Crash Course: U.S. Constitution: Introduction](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UYXaFVMY5uQ)
  - [Khan Academy: Introduction to Constitutional Law](https://www.khanacademy.org/humanities/law-and-politics/us-constitutional-law/a/introduction-to-constitutional-law)
- Task: Watch the Crash Course video and complete the Khan Academy's Introduction to Constitutional Law module. Write a one-page summary of the key principles and concepts you've learned, and discuss their importance in the context of a legal career.
